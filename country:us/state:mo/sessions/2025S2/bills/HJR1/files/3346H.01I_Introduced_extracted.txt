Section 52(b). The veto power of the governor shall not extend to measures referred

9 purposes of this section, the number of registered voters shall be determined by the
10 number of registered voters entitled to vote at the general, municipal, or primary
11 election, whichever is most recent, immediately preceding the election at which the
12 proposed constitutional amendment is to appear on the ballot. This section shall not be
13 construed to deprive any member of the general assembly of the right to introduce any
14 measure.

6 • Limit voting on initiative petitions to United States citizens;
7 • Reduce the number of signatures needed to place a proposed constitutional
8 amendment on the ballot by initiative petition; and
9 • Require a majority of Missouri registered voters to amend the constitution by
10 initiative petition?".

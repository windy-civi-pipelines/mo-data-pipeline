29 remedies.

31 petition on a statewide ballot measure is guilty of the crime of petition signature fraud:
32 (a) Signs any name other than his or her own to any petition, or who knowingly

50 exclusive criminal jurisdiction to prosecute under this section.
51 4. At a reasonable time and place after an initiative petition is submitted with
52 signatures and before a petition is placed on a ballot, the secretary of state or the
53 secretary's designee shall conduct one or more in-person or web-based hearings to
54 receive additional public comment regarding the purpose and effect of the proposed
55 measure. Transcriptsorsummariesofthehearingsshallbemadeavailabletothepublic
56 no later than seven days after the hearing is conducted and before the petition is placed
57 on the ballot.
58 5. NotwithstandingSections51and52(b)ofthisarticleandArticleXII,Section2
59 (b) of this constitution, statewide ballot measures proposed by the initiative are
60 approved only if affirmative votes are cast by a majority of voters in each congressional
61 district in effect at the time of the vote, notwithstanding any other provision of this
62 constitution to the contrary.
63 6. The full text of any statewide ballot measure proposed by the initiative shall
64 be made available to each voter, either legibly printed on paper or in digital format, at
65 the time a ballot is made available to the voter for voting. This requirement is in
66 addition to, and does not replace, all other printings and displays of the full text and the
67 ballot title required under this constitution or by law. The secretaryof state isexpressly
68 authorized to promulgate rules consistent with rule making authority under Missouri
69 law to implement and administer the provisions of this subsection.
70 7. The provisions of this section are self-executing. All of the provisions of this
71 section are severable. If any provision of this section is found by a court of competent
72 jurisdiction to be unconstitutional or unconstitutionally enacted, the remaining
73 provisions of this section shall be and remain valid.

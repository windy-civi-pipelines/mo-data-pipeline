6 contrary,the legislature shall be prohibited from attempting to repeal or amendanylawor
7 constitutional provision enactedbycitizeninitiative unlesseighty percent of legislatorsrefer the
8 change to voters. For the purposesof thissubsection,the phrase "attempting to repeal or amend
9 anylawor constitutional provision enactedbycitizeninitiative" shall be construedto mean
10 proposingor passinganylawor constitutional amendment that, if enacted,would modify, repeal,
11 or supersede,whether in whole or in part, andwhether explicitly or byimplication, anylawor

13 protect not only lawsandconstitutional provisionsenactedbycitizeninitiative after the effective

15 prior to the effective date of thissection.

17 31,32,and52(a) of thisconstitution, andanyother provision of lawor of thisconstitution to the

19 referendum power. The phrase "impede citizens'initiative or referendum power" shall be
20 construedbroadly including, but not limited to, raisingsignature thresholds,shorteningthe time
